Chicken stirfry

Ingredients:

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons stir-fry sauce
  • Cooked rice, for serving

stirfry

A quick and tasty stirfrired chicken any time.

View Recipe

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the chicken strips with soy sauce and cornstarch.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Add the bell peppers and onion to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender-crisp.
  5. Add the broccoli florets, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet. Pour in the stir-fry sauce and toss to coat everything evenly.
  7. Cook for 1-2 minutes more, until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  8. Serve hot over cooked rice.
