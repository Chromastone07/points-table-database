Easy Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs (e.g., thyme, oregano)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Simple Soup

A quick and tasty Soup for any night.

View Recipe

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and diced tomatoes. Stir in dried herbs.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot.
