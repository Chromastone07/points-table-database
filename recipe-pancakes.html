hot Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Simple pancake

A quick and tasty pancakes any time.

View Recipe

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the hot griddle, using approximately ¼ cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook for 3-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings (e.g., syrup, fruit).
